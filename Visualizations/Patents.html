Patents


Method of Estimating Well Disposal Capacity

A method for determining a maximum volume of drilling cuttings disposal in a formation, the method including inputting formation parameters into a simulator, simulating a formation during waste injection based on the formation parameters, determining a net pressure based on the simulating, determining a closure pressure increase based on the simulating, calculating a disposal volume based on the net pressure and the closure pressure, calculating a time interval of the waste injection based on the calculated injection volume, and outputting at least one of the disposal volume and the time interval is disclosed.

International Publication Number: WO 2009/105330 A2

Method of Using Pressure Signatures to Predict Injection Well Anomalies

A method of desiging a response to a fracture behavior of a formation during re-injection of cuttings into a formation, the method including obtaining a pressure signature for a time period, interpreting the pressure signature for the time perios to determine a fracture behavior of the formation, determining a solution based on the fracture behavior of the formation, and implememting a solution is disclosed.

Pub. No: US 2010/0314104 A1
